TAMBI TEA RESORT

8 - 9 JUNE, 2024

The 3rd edition of the Trail Running Competition in Dieng Plateau is back!

The "Dieng Caldera Race," with its stunning landscape and routes tailored to global standards, provides the best experience and training place for runners to 'level up' their trail running performance.

"With dedication to advancing the sport of trail running and elevating Indonesian trail runners to compete on the international stage, we are honored to announce the presence of representatives from UTMB (Ultra Trail du Mont Blanc), confirming our commitment to organizing international caliber events."

Join us in the third edition of the Dieng Caldera Race!
